eCommerce

It's no question, Shopify is #1 when it comes to eCommerce. It's my top recommendation for those looking to list their products online. I can help build your online store from start to finish!

1

Store Details

We will gather requirements: target market, products, branding assets, shipping zones, payment methods, and any integrations you might need. We will also discuss the strengths/weaknesses of Shopify, and other eCommerce platforms if necessary (Wix, Squarespace, or WooCommerce for Wordpress).

Web Design Web Development Ongoing Support
2

Setup

We will create your Shopify account and choose the plan of your choice. It's now time to configure your settings like the store name, domain, tax rules, payment, and shipping rates for your store. I will also show you around the Shopify interface since you will need to become very familiar with it when the store is live.

3

Design

With the configuration done, we shift to the design. Shopify's templating language powers every page, so it's important to consider that going with Shopify does sacrifice some customization for functionality. In this stage, we focus on colors, typography, layouts, and other brand assets.

Let's get started!
4

Testing

Lastly, we test every piece of your store from start to finish. We will place test orders, verify the checkout flow, confirm email notifications, test mobile responsiveness, and much more. Then it's time to remove the storefront password which will open your store for business!
