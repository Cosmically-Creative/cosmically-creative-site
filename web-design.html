Web Design

Your website doesn’t have to feel overwhelming. My design process is simple and collaborative, guiding you from the first idea to a finished site you’re proud to share. With a clear plan and open communication, this is how I can bring your vision to life step by step:

1

Discovery

We start with a brief meeting to capture your brand story, target audience, and project goals. From this I produce a clear design brief and a roadmap that outlines deliverables, timelines, and success metrics.

Web Development Ongoing Support eCommerce
2

Research

Using the brief, I conduct competitor analysis, gather inspiration, develop mood boards, color palettes, and typography options. This stage helps align visual direction with your brand personality before any layout work begins.

3

Wireframes

Now it's time to translate ideas into wireframes that map out page structure, navigation, and content. These are not meant to be pretty, but are very important in the next step.

Let's get started!
4

Prototypes

After your feedback, I evolve those into visual prototypes that showcase the final look and feel. With your approval on the final design, now it's time to give your website some life with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
